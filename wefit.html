Project Overview

WeFit is a mobile based application that encourages users to complete a set workout goal independently or interact with peers to accomplish the same goal. It aims to create a relaxed and enjoyable fitness platform with encouragement, sharing, and interest-based content to enhance reliance on and usage for the user.

Key Takeways

Based on my research, These already existing apps in the fitness category contain social networking functions, but no real-time fitness with peers. Although there are many fitness experts in the live broadcast of fitness content, the lack of interaction between viewers.




Persona

Define The Problem

The problems that most young people face are
Lack of visible results after exercise and difficulty in sticking to it Lack of motivation and support Lack of reminders on a daily basis No enough time to work out want to work out with peers, but schedule conflicts prevent it.

The problem I am trying to solve is
How might I enhance users' attraction and persistence to the app? What is the way to encourage each other?

These problems can be solved by
A fitness APP is designed to share the video instruction of fitness experts and professional trainers to guide users, analyze personal fitness progress, and have invitations and peers to participate in fitness in real-time to encourage each other. To follow the professional video training together, enhance motivation and mutual supervision.

The result can be seen by
App partners who use the real-time fitness feature are able to work out more consistently, which adds to the fun so they are willing to share and invite more peers to participate in workouts.
